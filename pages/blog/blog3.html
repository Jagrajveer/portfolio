    Threats from Password

    Password is a set of alphabets or numbers or symbols or a mixture of them that allows you to access your account. But some people hack the accounts of other people and misuse them. Thatâ€™s why making sure that the password does get into wrong is very important.

    How Passwords are Hacked?

    Hacking a password is done by many methods.

    • Login spoofing - Passwords are illegally collected through a fake page by cybercriminals
    • Sniffing attack - Password is stolen using illegal network access and with tools like key loggers.
    • Shoulder surfing attack - Stealing passwords when someone types them, at that time using a camera and gaining access to user data.
    • Brute force attack - Stealing passwords with the help of automated tools and gaining access to user data.
    • Data breach - Stealing login credentials and other confidential data directly from the website database.

    Traditional methods of password management

    Passwords were managed using writing them on paper and using only alphabets or numbers. Reusing the same passwords across different websites. These methods make hacking a password very easy because it takes very little time to crack them. Before people share passwords with an untrusted person.

    Modern methods of password management

    People use a mixture of alphabets, numbers, and symbols to create strong passwords. Reset passwords regularly and using two-factor authentication(2FA). 2FA is a method that helps to secure an account. It uses another pin or uses a mobile phone that receives a token every time an account is accessed. Store passwords in someplace safe and only share passwords with trusted people. Websites also provide a secure valet that saves your password in a secure place and stores them after encrypting them.

    Threats from hacking

    People with your passwords can access sensitive information. People can hack your bank account password or credit card password that can cause you a lot of trouble. If your social account gets hacked, then people with its password can use it to do bad things resulting in you get in trouble. The big company also suffers lots of damage if someone hacks it there network stealing sensitive information about the company or people using their services.
