    • About Me

    My name is Jagrajveer Singh. I grew up in a city named Ludhiana that is in Punjab, India. It is also known as the Manchester of India and is one of the biggest manufacturing hubs in India. There are five members in my family - my mother, father, grandmother, my younger sister, and myself.

    I enrolled in the Software Development Program at MITT because I love technology and programming. This course teaches the latest technology that will help me get the job I want. Also, there are many other career opportunities in the field of programming other than web development. The thing I enjoy about technology is the way it makes our lives easy and how magical it feels. I also like that all the electronics are now connected, making things more comfortable for me.

    From a young age, I was fascinated by technology. In high school, I learned about coding and got passionate about it. That's also the reason I choose this course. Other than the tech, I also like the gym and gaming.
